Lean In Org is an non-profit women empowerment organization by Sheryl Sandberg that 'Educate, Enable & Empower' the professional women. Inception since 2015, Lean In Malaysia was formed and it comprises of hunderds members with both women and men in the conversation as we strongly believe the gender equality does matter. In Malaysia, the organization is actively provide support platform such as Lean In MasterClass, Lean In Youth, Lean In Career Programme and Lean In Summit.

If you are interested, please visit Lean In Malaysia

Life At Work Award is an initiative by TalentCorp and Ministry of Human Resource. It is an entitlement for corporate companies and non-profit organization that prartice diversity and inclusion. It's about making the mix matter. Creating an inclusive enviroment for the society.
